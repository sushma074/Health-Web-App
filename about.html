Why need this Model..


--For individuals who have a disease, visiting the hospital for regular checkups is essential and highly beneficial.Early diagnosis allows timely treatment, increasing the chances of managing or curing the condition effectively.

Waste of Money:--Many people avoid hospital visits for disease prediction due to concerns about wasting money and time if they are found healthy. Financially, routine checkups often involve costly consultations and diagnostic tests. If no disease is detected, the expense may seem unnecessary.

Waste of Time:--Additionally, visiting hospitals requires time off from work or studies, long waiting times, and travel efforts, which can disrupt daily routines.


--Creating affordable, efficient, and time-saving healthcare solutions can help address these concerns and promote proactive health management.

--Keeping all this in mind we have created a Health-Prediction Web-App which predict whether u have any symptoms of the disease or not.

--In this Web-app we have also mentioned some precautions to prevent these Disease.

--Below we have mentioned some features of this Web-App.

Quick Prediction

We have used light ML model that can load within no time without compromising on the accuracy of the prediction. Light models quickly load which helps in better response of the page.

Wide Range

This Web-App provides a wide range of predicting the contamination multiple infectious disease like Kidney Disease, Liver Infection , Heart Disease, Diabetes and Breast Cancer.

Highly Accuracated

Though this app provides a wide range and high speed the accuracy has not been compromised. Hyper-parameters have been selected very carefully so that the accuracy is not hampered.
